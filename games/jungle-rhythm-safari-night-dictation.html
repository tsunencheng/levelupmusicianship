🌙 Jungle Rhythm Safari: Night Dictation

Listen closely in the dark — then tap back what you heard, by ear alone.
Rhythms: ta, ti-ti, tika-tika, ti-tika, tika-ti, too-oo. Best on a computer keyboard — tap SPACE in time with the beat. You won't see the rhythm while it plays — trust your ears!
