🌙 Jungle Rhythm Safari: Night Dictation

Listen closely in the dark — then tap back what you heard, by ear and by pitch alone.
Rhythms: ta, ti-ti, tika-tika, ti-tika, tika-ti, too-oo. Pitches: do re mi so la on keys 1 2 3 5 6. Best on a computer keyboard. You won't see the pattern while it plays — trust your ears!
